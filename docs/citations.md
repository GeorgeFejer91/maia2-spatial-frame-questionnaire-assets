# Citations And Source Links

Use these citations in study documentation and app metadata.

## Official MAIA Source

- UCSF Osher Center for Integrative Health. Multidimensional Assessment of Interoceptive Awareness. Official MAIA page: https://osher.ucsf.edu/research/maia
- Official German MAIA-2 PDF: https://osher.ucsf.edu/sites/osher.ucsf.edu/files/inline-files/MAIA-2_German.pdf
- Official English MAIA-2 PDF: https://osher.ucsf.edu/sites/osher.ucsf.edu/files/inline-files/MAIA2%202018.05.27.pdf

## MAIA-2 Development

- Mehling WE, Acree M, Stewart A, Silas J, Jones A (2018). The Multidimensional Assessment of Interoceptive Awareness, Version 2 (MAIA-2). PLoS ONE 13(12): e0208034. https://doi.org/10.1371/journal.pone.0208034

## German MAIA-2 Validation

- Eggart M, Todd J, Valdes-Stauber J (2021). Validation of the Multidimensional Assessment of Interoceptive Awareness (MAIA-2) questionnaire in hospitalized patients with major depressive disorder. PLoS ONE 16(6): e0253913. https://doi.org/10.1371/journal.pone.0253913
- S1 Questionnaire, German version of the MAIA-2 questionnaire: https://doi.org/10.1371/journal.pone.0253913.s001
- Figshare entry for the German questionnaire supplement: https://plos.figshare.com/articles/journal_contribution/German_version_of_the_MAIA-2_questionnaire_/14849357

## German MAIA Background, Not MAIA-2 Replacement

- Bornemann B, Herbert BM, Mehling WE, Singer T (2015). Differential changes in self-reported aspects of interoceptive awareness through 3 months of contemplative training. Frontiers in Psychology 5:1504. https://doi.org/10.3389/fpsyg.2014.01504
- Reis D (2017). Further Insights Into the German Version of the Multidimensional Assessment of Interoceptive Awareness (MAIA). European Journal of Psychological Assessment. https://doi.org/10.1027/1015-5759/a000404

## Recent Cross-Check

The 2024 Dutch MAIA-2 validation paper describes Eggart et al. as the German MAIA-2 validation, supporting that no newer German full MAIA-2 replacement was identified during the 2026-06-15 check:

- The Multidimensional Assessment of Interoceptive Awareness, version 2 (MAIA-2): psychometric properties in a Dutch non-clinical sample. https://doi.org/10.1186/s40359-024-01553-8
